It’s really simple! All players, except one, get the same location. One of the players gets the “Spy” role instead of a location. Then, players start asking each other free questions, trying to guess — who is the spy, by listening to answers. Questions may be: “Why are you dressed so strangely?” or “When was the last time we got a payday?” or anything else you can come up with!

The spy doesn’t know which location he is at, so he has to listen carefully. When it is his time to answer — he’d better create a good story! At any time during a round, one player may accuse another of being a spy. If all other players agree with the accusation, the round ends and the accused player reveals their identity. If the spy is uncovered, all other players win. However, the spy can end a round by announcing that he understands what the secret location is; if his guess is correct, only the spy wins.
